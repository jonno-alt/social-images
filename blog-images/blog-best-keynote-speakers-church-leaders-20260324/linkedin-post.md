Planning a church leadership conference or staff retreat?

Choosing the right keynote speaker is one of the most important decisions you will make. The wrong choice and your team walks away uninspired. The right choice and it becomes a catalyst for real change.

I put together a list of 50 of the best keynote speakers for church leaders in 2026, from megachurch pastors and bestselling authors to leadership consultants, executive coaches and emerging voices shaping the conversation around faith-based leadership.

The list includes names you would expect, and a few you might not.

Here are 3 things I noticed while researching this list:

1. The best church leadership speakers are not just great communicators. They are practitioners who have built teams, navigated conflict and made hard decisions.

2. There is a growing trend toward speakers who bring frameworks, not just inspiration. Tools like Working Genius, Boundaries, and Emotionally Healthy Leadership are transforming how church teams work together.

3. The most effective church leadership events pair a high-profile keynote with more specialised workshop facilitators for a well-rounded experience.
