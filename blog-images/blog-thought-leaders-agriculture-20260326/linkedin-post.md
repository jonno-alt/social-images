The agriculture industry is being reshaped by climate change, technology, and a global push toward regenerative practices.

So I put together a list of 50 thought leaders in agriculture worth following.

From regenerative farming pioneers like Gabe Brown and Allan Savory, to agtech CEOs building the robots and biological products transforming farms, to food systems leaders connecting policy with practice.

Three that stood out to me:

1. Gabe Brown turned his North Dakota ranch into a living laboratory for soil health. His five principles of regenerative agriculture have influenced farmers worldwide.

2. Danielle Nierenberg built Food Tank into one of the most important food systems think tanks on the planet, connecting farmers, scientists, and policymakers across 70+ countries.

3. Karsten Temme co-founded Pivot Bio to replace synthetic nitrogen fertiliser with microbial alternatives. Now used on millions of acres.
