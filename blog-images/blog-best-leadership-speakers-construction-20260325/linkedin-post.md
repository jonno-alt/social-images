I just published a list of the 50 best leadership speakers for construction companies in 2026.

Construction is one of the most demanding industries on the planet. Tight deadlines, thin margins, workforce shortages, safety pressures and generational shifts all happening at once.

The right leadership speaker can help your team see these challenges as opportunities.

Here are three things I learned while researching this list:

1. The best construction speakers understand that safety IS leadership. You cannot separate the two.

2. Lean thinking is transforming construction from the field up. The speakers driving this movement are building cultures, not just implementing tools.

3. Construction needs more diverse voices at the front of the room. The women and emerging leaders on this list are changing the industry for the better.
