The insurance industry is being reshaped by AI, climate risk, and digital distribution faster than most people realise.

So I put together a list of 50 thought leaders in insurance worth following in 2026.

It covers insurtech founders, global CEOs, consultants, podcast hosts, and emerging voices who are genuinely shaping the future of risk and insurance.

A few highlights from the list:

1. Paul Carroll built Insurance Thought Leadership into the industry's go-to platform with 1,500+ contributors and 5,000+ articles.

2. Nigel Walsh went from Deloitte to running insurance strategy at Google Cloud, and his take on digital transformation is always worth reading.

3. Matteo Carbone is the world's leading voice on IoT and insurance, and his data-driven analysis stands out from the noise.

4. Carey Anne Nadeau is tackling algorithmic fairness in insurance pricing through LOOP, which is some of the most important work happening in the industry right now.
