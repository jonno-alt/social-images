Who are the thought leaders shaping the future of mining in 2026? I put together a list of 50.

The mining industry is at a crossroads. The energy transition, critical minerals, autonomous operations, ESG accountability, and a global talent crunch are reshaping everything.

So I researched and wrote a guide to the 50 best thought leaders in mining, including major company CEOs, technology innovators, sustainability pioneers, investors, media voices, and emerging leaders from every continent.

A few highlights from the list:

Natascha Viljoen became the first woman to lead Newmont in its 100+ year history.

Robert Friedland continues to be described as the greatest mine developer of his generation, with Kamoa-Kakula set to become one of the world's biggest copper mines.

Dr Patience Mpofu, named the Pan African Most Influential Woman in Mining, is leading the conversation on ESG and circularity.
