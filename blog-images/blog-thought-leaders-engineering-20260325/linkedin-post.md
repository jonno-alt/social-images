I put together a list of 50 thought leaders in engineering worth following in 2026.

From software engineering management pioneers to aerospace innovators, robotics visionaries and engineering education advocates, these are the voices shaping how we build, lead and innovate.

Some highlights from the list:

Gergely Orosz runs The Pragmatic Engineer with 1.1M+ subscribers, the biggest engineering newsletter in the world.

Nicole Forsgren created the DORA framework that changed how we measure software delivery performance.

Lisa Su led one of the most remarkable turnarounds in semiconductor history at AMD.

Julia Evans makes complex engineering concepts accessible and fun through Wizard Zines.
