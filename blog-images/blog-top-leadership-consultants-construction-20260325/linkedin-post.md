Construction is one of the toughest industries to lead in. Tight margins, complex projects, safety risks, and a workforce that spans five generations.

Yet most construction companies still underinvest in leadership development.

I put together a list of 50 top leadership consultants for construction in 2026. These are the coaches, speakers, firms, and facilitators helping construction companies build leaders who can actually deliver.

A few standouts from the list:

1. FMI Corporation has been developing construction leaders for 70 years with 10,000+ alumni.
2. Eric Anderton at Construction Genius helps contractors from $10M to $1B shift from building projects to leading people.
3. Mark Breslin has delivered 2,000+ keynotes to 350,000+ construction professionals.
4. Wally Adamchik at FireStarter combines military discipline with construction expertise.
5. Bradley Hartmann has written 12 construction-focused books and hosts The Construction Leadership Podcast.
