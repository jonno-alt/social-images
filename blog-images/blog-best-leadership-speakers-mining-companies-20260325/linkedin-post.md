Who are the best leadership speakers for mining companies right now?

I spent weeks researching and put together a list of 50.

From safety culture experts to mining CEOs, neuroscientists to mental health advocates, this list covers every type of leadership speaker a mining company might need for their next conference, offsite or leadership day.

A few highlights:

Mark Cutifani CBE, 48+ years in mining, inducted into the US Mining Hall of Fame, challenges mining leaders to stop apologising for their industry.

Darren Flanagan, the man behind the Beaconsfield mine rescue, now shares his story of trauma, resilience and risk management with mining audiences across Australia.

Michelle Ash, former Chief Innovation Officer at Barrick Gold, one of the strongest voices on digital transformation in mining.

Michael Weston, 20 years in WA mining before a workplace stress collapse changed everything, now a Beyond Blue speaker on mental health in mining.
