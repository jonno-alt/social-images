I just published a guide to 50 top leadership consultants for agribusiness, and I am blown away by the depth of expertise in this space.

If you lead an agribusiness, whether that is a family farm, a cooperative, a food company, or a rural organisation, this list is for you.

Here are a few things that stood out during the research:

The best agribusiness leadership consultants are not just generic coaches who happen to work with farmers. They grew up on farms. They understand the rhythms of the season, the pressures of harvest, and the reality that your team might be spread across thousands of hectares.

Family succession is one of the biggest leadership challenges in agribusiness, and there are consultants who have built entire careers around helping farm families navigate those conversations.

The global consulting firms (McKinsey, Bain, BCG, Korn Ferry, Spencer Stuart) all have dedicated agribusiness practices, but some of the most impactful work is being done by individual coaches and smaller firms.
