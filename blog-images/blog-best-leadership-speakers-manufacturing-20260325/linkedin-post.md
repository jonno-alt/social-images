I put together a list of the 50 best leadership speakers for manufacturing in 2026.

Manufacturing is facing workforce shortages, digital disruption, and a generational shift in leadership expectations. The right speaker can shift the mindset of your entire leadership team.

Here are a few standouts from the list:

Mark Graban, 3x Shingo Award recipient, has spent 20+ years helping manufacturing teams build cultures where people speak up and improvement sticks.

Kathy Miller, Women in Manufacturing Hall of Fame inductee, started on the shop floor at 17 and now speaks on lean transformation and inclusive leadership.

Drew Crowe is championing The New American Manufacturing Renaissance, helping companies recruit and develop the next generation of manufacturing leaders.
