Healthcare is not short on voices. But genuine thought leaders who are shaping the future of the industry? That is a shorter list.

I put together a guide to the 50 best thought leaders in healthcare in 2026, covering digital health, patient safety, value-based care, health equity, nursing leadership, and more.

Some highlights from the list:

Eric Topol is redefining how we think about AI in medicine. Atul Gawande's "Checklist Manifesto" is still changing how hospitals operate. Amy Edmondson's work on psychological safety is transforming healthcare culture from the inside out. And Farzad Mostashari at Aledade is quietly reshaping how primary care gets paid for across America.
