Who are the best leadership speakers for aged care in 2026?

I just published the most comprehensive list I could put together.

50 speakers. CEOs, consultants, psychologists, regulators, innovators, and international voices who understand the unique pressures of leading in aged care.

Here are a few that stood out during the research:

Kelvin Neave at Dougherty Apartments was named Leader of the Year at the 2025 Future of Ageing Awards. His staff retention rate? 95-96%. That is not a typo.

Cynthia Payne at Anchor Excellence has worked with over 300 aged care providers and impacted 45% of the market.

Graeme Cowan, founding board director of R U OK?, has spent 20 years helping leaders build resilient teams. His new book "Great Leaders Care" launches this year.

The full list covers everyone from peak body CEOs to digital transformation leaders to specialist aged care psychologists.
