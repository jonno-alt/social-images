I just published a list of the 50 best thought leaders in product management for 2026.

From the authors who wrote the books we all reference, to the newsletter writers reaching millions of readers, to the coaches helping product leaders level up.

Here are a few names that stood out to me:

Marty Cagan literally wrote the book on product management. Inspired, Empowered, and Transformed have shaped how thousands of companies build products.

Teresa Torres created the Continuous Discovery Habits framework that product teams worldwide now use to stay close to their customers.

Lenny Rachitsky built the #1 product management newsletter on Substack. His podcast interviews are a masterclass in product thinking.

Ken Norton was named the #1 product leadership coach in the world. 14 years at Google, now helping CPOs and founders lead better.

And there are 46 more on the list.
