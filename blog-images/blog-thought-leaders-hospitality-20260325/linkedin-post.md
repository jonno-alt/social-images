The biggest challenges in hospitality are rarely about the product.

They are about people.

Team dynamics. Communication breakdowns. Leadership development. Getting partners aligned. Building cultures that retain talent.

These are not hospitality problems. They are leadership problems.

I put together a list of 50 thought leaders in hospitality who are solving them, from legendary hoteliers to customer service experts, technology innovators, and the women reshaping the industry.

Here are a few standouts:

Danny Meyer redefined restaurant hospitality with "enlightened hospitality" and Setting the Table.

Horst Schulze built The Ritz-Carlton's legendary service standards from scratch.

Will Guidara turned Eleven Madison Park into the world's best restaurant and wrote Unreasonable Hospitality.

Chip Conley grew Joie de Vivre into America's second largest boutique hotel brand, then mentored Airbnb's founders.

Shep Hyken has written 8 NYT bestsellers on customer service and experience.

Emily Goldfischer founded hertelier and has interviewed 600+ women in hospitality leadership.

Josiah Mackenzie hosts the #1 hospitality podcast, Hospitality Daily.
