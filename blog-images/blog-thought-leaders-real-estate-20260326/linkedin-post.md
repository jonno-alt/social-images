I put together a list of 50 thought leaders in real estate worth following in 2026.

From CEOs who have reshaped how we buy and sell property, to coaches who have helped thousands of agents build thriving businesses, to investors managing billions in global portfolios, these are the voices shaping real estate right now.

Here are a few standouts from the list:

Robert Reffkin built Compass into the world's largest residential brokerage and is ranked #1 on the Swanepoel Power 200 for the second year running.

Barbara Corcoran turned a $1,000 loan into a $5 billion real estate empire before becoming one of the most recognised investors on Shark Tank.

Tom Ferry has been ranked the #1 real estate coach for four consecutive years, with over 35,000 hours of coaching experience.

Brandon Turner taught millions how to invest in real estate through BiggerPockets and now manages a $650M portfolio through Open Door Capital.
