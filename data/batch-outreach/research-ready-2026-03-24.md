**1. Stuartholme School**
Stuartholme is Brisbane's leading independent Catholic girls' school (Years 5-12), located at 365 Birdwood Terrace, Toowong. Founded in 1920 by the Society of the Sacred Heart, it is part of an international network of 200+ Sacred Heart schools across 44 countries. The school caters for approximately 700 students including 150 boarders, making it Brisbane's only Catholic girls' boarding school. Set across 21 hectares of heritage-listed grounds.
